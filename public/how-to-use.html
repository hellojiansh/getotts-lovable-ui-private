Common Questions

What if my account stops working?

Contact us on WhatsApp immediately. We'll replace your account within 24 hours or issue a full refund. Your warranty covers the entire duration of your plan.

Can I use the account on multiple devices?

How do I renew my subscription?

Before your plan expires, simply visit GetOTTs and purchase the same product again. We'll send you fresh credentials. You can also set a reminder via our WhatsApp bot.
